Welcome to Montana - Big Sky Country


Montana is a landlocked state in the mountain west region of the United States and is the 43rd most populated state while being the 4th largest state in the union. Montana first came under American sovereignty with the Louisiana Purchase from France in 1803 and entered statehood on November 8th, 1889. If you are interested in more information about Montana, please visit Montana Brief History.
